About me

Hello, my name is André Luiz, I am a Computer Scientist with experience as a Software Engineer, Data Engineer, and Data Scientist in multiple industries: Edtech, Public Prosecution and Court of Law, Oil and gas Distribution, and Agribusiness.

I describe myself as a software engineer working with data. I have a solid academic background in Computer Science, with a Ph.D. in Informatics (2020) from the Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio) in the Data Science field, and a M.Sc. (2015) and a Bachelor’s degrees (2012) in Computer Science are from the Universidade Federal do Maranhão (UFMA) in Brazil in the Multimedia field.

My strong interests are software engineering, data engineering, data science, and machine learning.

Currently, I work as a Data Engineer at Seedz, a Vertical SaaS to connect and optimize the agribusiness ecosystem.
