About me

Hello, my name is André Luiz, I am computer scientist with and I have experience as a developer in multimedia, data science, and machine learning. I have a solid academic background, with a Ph.D. in computer science (2020) from the Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio) in the Data Science field. My M.Sc. (2015) and a Bachelor’s degrees (2012) in computer science are from the Universidade Federal do Maranhão (UFMA) in Brazil in the Multimedia field. Currently, I work as Data Scientist at ExACTa – PUC-Rio, where I perform projects that uses artificial intelligence, data analysis, data exploration, real-time analytics, and process mining to Petrobras, a large company of Oil & Gas in Brazil. My last job was as Data Scientist and Software Developer at ECOA – PUC-Rio. I have worked in analysis and exploration of data from Public Prosecution and Court of Law of Rio de Janeiro, to identify patterns in the court proceedings and development of prediction models to increase the efficiency in the legal processes. My educational background has an emphasis on software development, data science, machine learning, and multimedia systems.
