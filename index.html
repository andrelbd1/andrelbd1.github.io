About me

Hello, my name is André Luiz, I am Computer Scientist and I have experience as Software Engineer, and Data Scientist in multiple industries: Edtech, Public Prosecution and Court of Law, Oil & Gas Distribution, and Agribusiness.

I have a solid academic background, with a Ph.D. in Informatics (2020) from the Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio) in the Data Science field. My M.Sc. (2015) and a Bachelor’s degrees (2012) in Computer Science are from the Universidade Federal do Maranhão (UFMA) in Brazil in the Multimedia field.

My strong interests are in software engineering, data science, machine learning, and data engineering.

Currently, I work as Senior Data Scientist, and Manager at Seedz, a Vertical SaaS to connect and optimize the agribusiness ecosystem.
